What does LCM stand for?

    Lowest Common Multiplier

Consider the following code:

    import numpy as np
    num1 = 4
    num2 = 6
    x = np.lcm(num1, num2)

What will be the result of x?

    12

What is a correct method to use when finding the Lowest Common Multiple of 
all values in an array?

    np.lcm.reduce()
